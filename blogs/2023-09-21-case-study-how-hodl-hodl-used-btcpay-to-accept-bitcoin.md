Ensuring attendees had access to bitcoin for making purchases was paramount. BTCPay Server checkout page is compatible with NFC with tap to pay. Thanks to LNBits and PlebTag over 500 NFC voucher cards have been created and pre-loaded with approximately 21$ USD worth of bitcoin.
